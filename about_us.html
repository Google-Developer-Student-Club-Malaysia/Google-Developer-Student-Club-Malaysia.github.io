About Us

The Machine Learning Study Jam is a community-run study group organized by Google Developer Student Club Malaysia in collaboration with multiple Google Developer Student Clubs of different chapters including the University of Malaya, Universiti Sains Malaysia, Universiti Putra Malaysia, Universiti Utara Malaysia, Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka, Universiti Kuala Lumpur, and Sunway University. It aims to provide a hands-on Machine Learning experience for our participants as inclusive as possible with our student mentors. Ranging from basic Machine Learning introductory sessions to performing hands-on Machine Learning projects from scratch, this study jam is open to all university students in Malaysia who are interested and want to know more about the features and capabilities of Machine Learning using Google Technologies. The Machine Learning Study Jam will cover topics ranging from Deep Learning to Sentiment Analysis. Participants will get the opportunity to explore the fundamentals of Machine Learning and to dive further into machine learning tools such as TensorFlow to develop niche machine learning skills.
