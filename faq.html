F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is study Jam?

    Study Jams are study groups organised by Google Developer Groups (GDG) worldwide. In these study groups, the participants will get personal/small group tutoring by mentors, study with peers and will receive a certificate upon completion.

  • How is ML Study Jam different from other Machine Learning Workshops?

    We provide small group mentor-mentee teaching, and we highly encourage participants to ask questions and actively engage with mentors.

  • How can I join this ML Study Jam? / Step to join us

    You can fill in the registration form provided here, and if you are not sure which group you belong to, feel free to do an assessment on the website and read the prerequisite for each level group.

  • Who can participate in the ML Study Jam?/ Which university’s student is Machine Learning Study Jam available in?/ I am not a university student but working, can I join ?/ I am not from a Computer Science background? Can I join ?

    Anyone with a high passion to learn Machine Learning/Deep learning and able to commit for the 3-days long study jam are welcome.

  • Do I need to pay to register for the Study Jam?

    No. You do not have to pay anything to anyone to register yourself for the GDSC Study Jam. It's totally free for you to join and learn!

  • Do I need to have any specific qualifications to join the Study Jam?

    No, but for better understanding, we highly recommend you to do an assessment test on the website and read the prerequisite for each level group to find the most suitable level group.

  • What is the gift ? /Will I get any certificate/gift after completing the Study Jam?

    Yes, you will get a certificate of completion if you attend the study jam fully.

  • Any self preparation before joining the workshop?/ Do I need to download any software in advance before the Study Jam?/ Anything to prepare before Study Jam?

    No, we will utilise google resources such as google colab for the study jam which don’t require you to download any software in advance. Be sure that you have Zoom in your device.

  • Is the study jam held online?

    Yes, the study jam is fully online.
